<p>Mark Harris recently <a href="http://www.vimeo.com/8944337">recorded</a> Udi Dahan talking about CQRS when he was at a user group in Australia.</p> <p>It’s about 90 minutes long, but if you’re trying to wrap your head around CQRS, it’s a great place to start.</p> <p>One other note, Udi’s style is independent of “event sourcing” as a mechanism for storing and rehydrating your objects from persistence.</p>
